InGodot Community Code of Conduct
We are a community centered around Godot and game development.
Our goal is to maintain a comfortable, respectful, and helpful environment for everyone.
To achieve this, we have agreed on the following rules:
» Communication
Neutrality: We do not discuss politics, religion, or ideology to avoid unnecessary conflict.
Sensitivity: We avoid heavy or sensitive topics that may distress other members.
Gender & Identity: We do not discuss gender issues or gender politics.
Zero Tolerance: Racism and any form of discrimination are strictly prohibited.
» Content
No NSFW: We do not post "Not Safe For Work" (adult) content.
Standard Material: We avoid shocking, violent, or provocative content.
Safety: The distribution of "doxing" material, extremism, or any illegal content is forbidden.
» Atmosphere
Respect: Communication must be respectful. No insults or toxicity.
Harmony: Do not provoke conflicts or incite arguments.
Language: Profanity should be used sparingly and only for emotional emphasis, not for abuse.
» Order
No Spam: Do not spam stickers, GIFs, images, or advertisements.
No Excessive Flooding: Avoid off-topic discussions (except in the designated Off-topic section) and refrain from flooding the chat.
AI Debates: We do not engage in arguments regarding Artificial Intelligence to prevent "holy wars" from taking over the chat.
Note: If a discussion gets out of hand, the moderation team will intervene.
» Community Structure
Organization: We strive to keep topic threads clean.
Correct Channels: Use the designated sections for their intended purposes.
Showcase: Share your projects and game dev posts specifically in the Gallery section.
Relevancy: Content unrelated to Godot or supporting tools may be removed.
» Moderation
We understand that mistakes happen. Therefore, we follow a simple progression:
Warning → Mute → Ban
Note: In cases of systematic flooding, the duration of the mute will increase progressively.
