# InGodot Community Code of Conduct

We are a community centered around Godot and game development. 
Our goal is to maintain a **comfortable, respectful, and helpful** environment for everyone.

To achieve this, we have agreed on the following:

### » Communication
* **Neutrality:** We do not discuss politics, religion, or ideology to avoid unnecessary conflict.
* **Sensitivity:** We avoid heavy or sensitive topics that may distress other members.
* **Gender & Identity:** We do not discuss gender issues or gender politics.
* **Zero Tolerance:** Racism and any form of discrimination are strictly prohibited.

### » Content
* **No NSFW:** We do not post NSFW (adult) content.
* **Standard Material:** We avoid shocking, violent, or provocative content.
* **Safety:** The distribution of doxing, extremism, or similar harmful material is forbidden.

### » Atmosphere
* **Respect:** Communication must be respectful, without insults or toxicity.
* **Harmony:** We do not provoke conflicts or incite arguments.
* **Language:** Profanity should be used moderately and only for emotional emphasis.

### » Order
* **No Spam:** Do not spam stickers, GIFs, images, or advertisements.
* **No Excessive Flooding:** Avoid off-topic discussions (except in the designated Off-topic section) and refrain from flooding.
* **AI Debates:** We do not engage in arguments regarding AI to prevent "holy wars" from taking over the chat.

> *Note: If a discussion goes beyond these boundaries, moderation may intervene.*

### » Community Structure
* **Organization:** We strive to keep topic threads clean and organized.
* **Correct Channels:** Use the designated sections for their intended purposes.
* **Showcase:** Share your projects and game dev posts in the **Gallery** section.
* **Relevancy:** Content unrelated to Godot or supporting tools may be removed.

### » Moderation
We understand that mistakes happen, so we follow a simple system:
**Warning → Mute → Ban**

*Note: In cases of systematic flooding, the duration of the mute will increase progressively.*
